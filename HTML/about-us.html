Cauliflower Culture

We've all seen a Cauliflower Ear stemming from high intensity sports, which are instantly recognizable - bringing strangers together for a brief second as they share a story. After the external portion of the ear has suffered a serious blow, blood begins to form, often leaving the ear permanently disfigured with an excessive growth of tissue. The ear is transformed into a number of sizes, shapes, and colors, taking the appearance of a flattened prune or cauliflower. Each Cauliflower Ear bares the scars of one's journey, hard work, pain, and discipline.

At Cauliflower Culture, we are bound to the few individuals who have endured the pain, sweat, blood, and tears of each broken ear. This pain proves one's dedication to the sport and the necessary sacrifices that must be made. We understand this unique discipline and passion, no matter if it be boxing, wrestling, MMA, Rugby, Judo, Jiu-Jitsu, Muay Thai, or Sumo. In the end, we all belong to the same culture and lifestyle.
